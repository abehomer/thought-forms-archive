// SYSTEM_OVERVIEW
MATERIALIZING THE INNER SIGNAL
ARCHIVE_STATEMENT.LOG

Thought Forms is a brain–computer interface (BCI) project that translates human brain activity into dynamic, generative visual forms. Developed in TouchDesigner, the system uses a particle aggregation algorithm that responds in real time to EEG data collected from a wearable headset. Variations in brainwave signals associated with emotional and cognitive states such as stress, attention, engagement, and excitement directly influence the behavior, density, and structure of the evolving form.

The resulting visualization functions as a quantifiable, externalized representation of internal mental states, allowing users to see otherwise invisible patterns of emotion and cognition. Beyond visualization, these forms can be captured and 3D printed, transforming ephemeral brain activity into tangible artifacts. In therapeutic and reflective contexts, Thought Forms offers a novel tool for mental health therapies, biofeedback, and self-awareness, enabling users and clinicians to track emotional states over time through both digital and physical outputs.

01
EEG Signal Capture
Wearable EEG headsets translate brainwave activity into measurable emotional metrics in real time: stress, interest, engagement, excitement, attention, relaxation.
02
Particle Aggregation
Emotional metrics drive particle simulations. Each value modulates force fields, density, velocity, and clustering behavior, creating a unique generative form.
03
Material Artifact
Particle clouds are captured as 3D models and in select cases 3D printed, transforming ephemeral cognitive states into tangible, archivable objects.
04
Living Archive
This interface serves as a digital reliquary; a permanent home for each thought form, with the emotional data and narrative context of its creation.

INPUT_LAYER
EEG & Metrics

A wearable EEG device continuously streams brainwave data. An SDK outputs six emotional indices — each normalized to 0–100 — sampled multiple times per second.

GENERATIVE_LAYER
Particle Engine

Emotional output metrics map to particle system parameters in real time. Each emotion drives a corresponding color and density output, resulting in a unique aggregate form.

OUTPUT_LAYER
3D Capture & Print

When a resonant configuration emerges it is frozen and exported as a mesh. Select specimens are prepared for 3D printing — physical artifacts of the cognitive state.
